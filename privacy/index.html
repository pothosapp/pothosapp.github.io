Privacy Policy

Effective / last updated: July 14, 2026

Voke is a quote app. This policy describes exactly what data Voke collects, why, and what it does not collect.

The only data Voke ever sends off your device

Voke includes an in-app feedback form. If you choose to submit feedback, the text you type — along with the category you selected (General, Feature Request, Content Request, or Bug Report) — is sent to a private messaging channel we use to read and respond to feedback.

That is the only network request Voke makes with any user-provided content. We do not attach your name, device identifiers, location, timestamps, or any other information to that message. If you don't use the feedback form, nothing about you is ever transmitted anywhere.

Feedback submissions are not linked to your identity and are not used to track you across sessions or devices.

Data that stays on your device

Voke stores the following information locally, on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or to any third party:

  • Your reading streak and reading history
  • Your profile name and age, if you choose to enter them
  • Your recent search history within the app
  • Any quotes you write and save yourself
  • Your widget and rotation preferences

This local data may be shared between the Voke app and its widget on your device (via an iOS App Group), but it never leaves your device.

We do not sell or share your data

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes. We have no advertising partners and no data-broker relationships.

What Voke does not do

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising, and no advertising identifiers are collected
  • No third-party SDKs beyond the single feedback channel described above
  • No user accounts, sign-in, or passwords
  • No use of Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, because Voke does not track you
  • No access to your location, contacts, health data, photos, camera, or microphone

"Do Not Track" signals

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Voke does not track users in any form, so there is no tracking for a DNT signal to disable — the app behaves the same regardless of any such setting.

Children's privacy

Voke is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because Voke does not collect identifying information at all, this applies equally regardless of the user's age.

Changes to this policy

If Voke's data practices change, this page will be updated and the "Effective / last updated" date above will change accordingly. For material changes, we will also show a notice in the app the next time a relevant update is available.

Developer & contact

Voke is developed by Kingston Preest, based in Glendale, Arizona, USA. Questions or complaints about this policy can be sent through the feedback form inside the Voke app — that is the channel we monitor for exactly this purpose.
